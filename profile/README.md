# Digital Skills Agency

Welcome to the Digital Skills Agency - experts in digital skills.

We help our clients understand what digital skills they have, what skills they need, and how to manage they digital capability and teams in the new world of work. 

Because skills matter to us, we are an Accredited Partner of the SFIA Foundation, and we work extensively with the SFIA Framework and contribute to its development.

## What we do

- **SFIA Services** — we provide accredited assessments, and accredited training as well as job mapping, evaluation of learning resources and support for integrating SFIA into your people practices.
- **Organisation and team design** — we have deep expertise in designing organisation and team changes, writing high-quality position descriptions, and aligning workforce plans to organisational strategy.
- **Digital team performance consulting** — we're here to help your digital team reach their potential, through training and support for people leaders, one-on-one coaching, and operating model/process improvements.
